Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to operate our property marketing platform: property data (descriptions, amenities, photos, videos), social media account credentials (stored encrypted), guest inquiry information (name, email, message), and analytics data (click counts, geographic location of link visitors).

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to generate marketing content for properties, process and optimize media for social publishing, publish approved content to authorized social media accounts, track marketing campaign performance, and manage guest inquiries.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services: Amazon Web Services (infrastructure), Google Gemini (AI content generation), Amazon Polly (text-to-speech), TikTok Content Posting API (video publishing), X/Twitter API (social publishing), and MaxMind GeoLite2 (IP geolocation for analytics).

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on Amazon Web Services infrastructure in the US East (Virginia) region. Sensitive credentials are stored encrypted using AWS Systems Manager Parameter Store. Media files are stored in Amazon S3 with server-side encryption.

5. Data Retention

Content cache items expire after 24 hours (configurable). Guest inquiries are retained for one year. Analytics data is retained indefinitely for reporting. Media files are retained until manually removed.

6. Social Media Data

When you authorize our platform to access your TikTok or X account, we store OAuth tokens securely and use them solely to publish content you have approved. We do not access your followers, messages, or other account data beyond what is required for content publishing.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through the platform's inquiry system.
